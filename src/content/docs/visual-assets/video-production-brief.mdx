# Video Production Brief & Shot List

*Crowdfunding Campaign Video Strategy*

## Production Overview

**Campaign Goal**: Raise $500K+ through crowdfunding with video as primary conversion driver  
**Target Audience**: Tech-savvy professionals and families (ages 30-55, household income $75K+)  
**Brand Voice**: Sophisticated, calm, confident, trustworthy  
**Key Message**: "Your Home, In Sync" - intelligent guardian for modern living

## Video Content Strategy

### Primary Campaign Video (Hero Video)
**Duration**: 2-3 minutes  
**Purpose**: Main conversion driver for campaign page  
**Style**: Cinematic lifestyle with product integration  
**Tone**: Aspirational, sophisticated, emotionally resonant

### Supporting Video Content
1. **Product Demonstration Videos** (30-60 seconds each)
2. **Customer Testimonial Videos** (60-90 seconds each)
3. **Feature Spotlight Videos** (15-30 seconds each)
4. **Social Media Content** (15-60 seconds each)
5. **Behind-the-Scenes Content** (30-90 seconds each)

## Main Campaign Video Brief

### Creative Concept
**Theme**: "A Day in the Life with HeyZack"  
**Narrative Arc**: Follow a family through 24 hours showing seamless HeyZack integration  
**Emotional Journey**: Stress → Relief → Confidence → Satisfaction

### Video Structure (2:30 total)

**Opening Hook (0:00-0:15)**
- Problem setup: Fragmented smart home frustrations
- Emotional hook: Family dealing with complexity
- Transition: "What if your home just worked?"

**Solution Introduction (0:15-0:45)**
- HeyZack brand introduction
- Core value proposition
- "Your Home, In Sync" tagline

**Benefit Demonstration (0:45-1:45)**
- Security pillar showcase (30 seconds)
- Comfort pillar showcase (30 seconds)
- Savings pillar showcase (30 seconds)

**Social Proof & Trust (1:45-2:15)**
- Beta user testimonials
- Expert endorsements
- Awards and recognition

**Call to Action (2:15-2:30)**
- Campaign urgency
- Backing incentives
- Clear next steps

**Color Palette**:
- **Primary**: HeyZack Charcoal (#1D1D1B)

## Technical Production Specifications

### Camera Requirements

**Primary Camera**: 
- **Model**: Sony FX6 or equivalent
- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps (cinematic), 60fps (smooth motion)
- **Codec**: Sony XAVC-I or ProRes 422 HQ
- **Color Profile**: S-Log3 for maximum flexibility

**Secondary Camera**:
- **Model**: Sony A7S III or equivalent
- **Purpose**: B-roll, close-ups, handheld shots
- **Resolution**: 4K matching primary
- **Stabilization**: In-body or gimbal required

**Drone Camera**:
- **Model**: DJI Air 2S or equivalent
- **Purpose**: Establishing shots, property overviews
- **Resolution**: 4K, matching color profile
- **Flight Time**: Minimum 25 minutes per battery

### Audio Requirements

**Primary Audio**:
- **Recorder**: Zoom F6 or Sound Devices MixPre-6
- **Microphones**: Shotgun (Sennheiser MKE 600)
- **Wireless**: Sennheiser G4 system for interviews
- **Monitoring**: Professional headphones required

**Backup Audio**:
- **Camera Audio**: Always record as backup
- **Sync Method**: Timecode or clap sync
- **Quality**: 48kHz/24-bit minimum
- **Redundancy**: Dual recording recommended

### Lighting Equipment

**Key Lighting**:
- **LED Panels**: Aputure 300D II or equivalent
- **Softboxes**: Various sizes for different shots
- **Color Temperature**: 5600K daylight balanced
- **Control**: Wireless control for efficiency

**Fill and Accent**:
- **Smaller LEDs**: Aputure 120D II series
- **Practical Lights**: Table lamps, ceiling fixtures
- **Reflectors**: 5-in-1 reflector kit
- **Flags**: Black flags for light control

### Post-Production Workflow

**Editing Software**:
- **Primary**: Adobe Premiere Pro CC
- **Color**: DaVinci Resolve Studio
- **Graphics**: Adobe After Effects CC
- **Audio**: Adobe Audition CC

**File Management**:
- **Storage**: Redundant backup system
- **Organization**: Consistent folder structure
- **Versioning**: Clear version control system
- **Archive**: Long-term storage solution

**Delivery Specifications**:
- **Master**: 4K ProRes 422 HQ
- **Campaign**: 1080p H.264 high quality
- **Social**: Platform-specific optimizations
- **Backup**: Multiple format versions
