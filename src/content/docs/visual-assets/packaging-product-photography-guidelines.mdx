# Packaging & Product Photography Guidelines

Professional photography and packaging guidelines to create compelling visual assets for your crowdfunding campaign.

## Photography Strategy Overview

### Visual Hierarchy for Crowdfunding

#### Primary Images (Hero Shots)
- **Purpose**: First impression and main campaign image
- **Usage**: Campaign header, social media, press releases
- **Requirements**: High-resolution, professional lighting, clean background
- **Quantity**: 3-5 variations for A/B testing

#### Secondary Images (Feature Focus)
- **Purpose**: Highlight specific features and benefits
- **Usage**: Campaign gallery, product descriptions, ads
- **Requirements**: Detail shots, lifestyle context, comparison views
- **Quantity**: 8-12 images covering all key features

#### Lifestyle Images (Context)
- **Purpose**: Show product in real-world usage
- **Usage**: Social media, email marketing, testimonials
- **Requirements**: Natural settings, diverse users, authentic scenarios
- **Quantity**: 10-15 images across different use cases

#### Technical Images (Specifications)
- **Purpose**: Demonstrate functionality and build trust
- **Usage**: FAQ sections, technical specifications, comparisons
- **Requirements**: Clear details, measurements, component views
- **Quantity**: 5-8 technical documentation shots

## Product Photography Guidelines

### Equipment Requirements

#### Camera Setup
- **Camera**: DSLR or mirrorless with manual controls
- **Lens**: 50mm or 85mm prime lens (minimal distortion)
- **Tripod**: Sturdy tripod for consistent framing
- **Remote**: Cable release or wireless trigger

#### Lighting Equipment
- **Key Light**: Large softbox or umbrella (main light source)
- **Fill Light**: Smaller softbox or reflector (shadow reduction)
- **Background Light**: Strip light or reflector (separation)
- **Accent Light**: Small LED or flash (highlight details)

#### Studio Setup
- **Background**: Seamless white paper or cyclorama
- **Surface**: White acrylic, wood, or textured materials
- **Props**: Minimal, brand-appropriate accessories
- **Space**: Minimum 10x10 feet for flexibility

### Camera Settings

#### Technical Specifications
- **File Format**: RAW + JPEG
- **Resolution**: Minimum 24MP (6000x4000 pixels)
- **Aperture**: f/8 to f/11 (optimal sharpness)
- **ISO**: 100-400 (minimize noise)
- **Shutter Speed**: 1/125s or faster
- **White Balance**: Daylight (5600K) or custom

#### Focus and Composition
- **Focus Mode**: Single-point autofocus or manual
- **Focus Stacking**: For extreme close-ups
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines, symmetry
- **Framing**: Leave 20% margin for cropping flexibility

### Lighting Setups

#### Setup 1: Clean Product Shot
**Purpose**: Hero images, catalog shots, clean backgrounds

**Lighting Configuration**:
- **Key Light**: 45° angle, camera left, large softbox
- **Fill Light**: Camera right, reflector or small softbox
- **Background Light**: Behind product, strip light on background
- **Ratio**: 3:1 key to fill light ratio

**Camera Position**: Eye level with product center
**Background**: Pure white seamless
**Props**: None or minimal brand elements

#### Setup 2: Dramatic Feature Shot
**Purpose**: Highlight specific features, create visual interest

**Lighting Configuration**:
- **Key Light**: 60° angle, camera left, focused beam
- **Rim Light**: Behind product, creates edge separation
- **Fill Light**: Minimal, maintain shadow detail
- **Accent Light**: Small LED to highlight key feature

**Camera Position**: Slightly above or below eye level
**Background**: Gradient or textured surface
**Props**: Complementary elements that enhance the story

#### Setup 3: Lifestyle Context
**Purpose**: Show product in real-world environment

**Lighting Configuration**:
- **Natural Light**: Large window or outdoor shade
- **Fill Flash**: Bounce flash to balance shadows
- **Reflector**: White or silver to control contrast
- **Ambient**: Maintain natural lighting feel

**Camera Position**: Natural perspective for use case
**Background**: Real environment, controlled clutter
**Props**: Authentic lifestyle elements

### Shot List Templates

#### Hero Shot Variations
1. **Straight-on Product**: Front view, centered, clean background
2. **Three-Quarter Angle**: Shows depth and dimension
3. **Top-Down Flat Lay**: Organized layout with accessories
4. **Hand-Held Scale**: Shows size relationship with human hand
5. **Packaging Included**: Product with retail packaging

#### Feature Detail Shots
1. **Close-up Details**: Macro shots of key features
2. **Component Breakdown**: Exploded or separated parts
3. **Size Comparison**: Next to common objects (coin, phone)
4. **Material Texture**: Surface details and finish quality
5. **Interface/Controls**: Buttons, screens, interaction points

#### Lifestyle Usage Shots
1. **Primary Use Case**: Most common usage scenario
2. **Alternative Uses**: Secondary applications
3. **User Demographics**: Different age groups, genders
4. **Environment Variety**: Home, office, outdoor, travel
5. **Group Usage**: Multiple people using product

#### Technical Documentation
1. **Dimensions**: Ruler or measuring tape visible
2. **Weight Demonstration**: Scale or comparison
3. **Durability Testing**: Stress tests, drop tests
4. **Assembly Process**: Step-by-step construction
5. **Maintenance**: Cleaning, storage, care instructions

## Packaging Design Guidelines

### Packaging Strategy

#### Unboxing Experience Design
- **First Impression**: Premium materials and construction
- **Reveal Sequence**: Logical progression of discovery
- **Brand Reinforcement**: Consistent visual identity
- **Social Media Ready**: Instagram-worthy unboxing moments

#### Functional Requirements
- **Protection**: Secure product during shipping
- **Sustainability**: Eco-friendly materials when possible
- **Cost Efficiency**: Balance premium feel with budget
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Safety warnings, certifications

### Packaging Photography

#### Unboxing Sequence Shots
1. **Sealed Package**: As received by customer
2. **Opening Moment**: First glimpse of contents
3. **Contents Revealed**: Full layout of included items
4. **Product Extraction**: Removing main product
5. **Complete Setup**: Everything unpacked and arranged

#### Packaging Detail Shots
1. **Material Quality**: Texture and finish details
2. **Brand Elements**: Logo, typography, color accuracy
3. **Information Hierarchy**: Instructions, warnings, features
4. **Sustainability Features**: Recycling symbols, eco-materials
5. **Premium Touches**: Embossing, foil, special finishes

### Brand Consistency

#### Visual Identity Elements
- **Logo Placement**: Consistent positioning and sizing
- **Color Palette**: Exact brand color reproduction
- **Typography**: Brand fonts and hierarchy
- **Imagery Style**: Consistent with brand personality

#### Photography Style Guide
- **Color Grading**: Consistent post-processing approach
- **Contrast Levels**: Maintain brand aesthetic
- **Saturation**: Natural vs. enhanced based on brand
- **Mood**: Professional, approachable, innovative, etc.

## Post-Production Workflow

### RAW Processing

#### Basic Adjustments
1. **Exposure Correction**: Optimize overall brightness
2. **White Balance**: Ensure accurate color temperature
3. **Highlight Recovery**: Retain detail in bright areas
4. **Shadow Lifting**: Reveal detail in dark areas
5. **Contrast Enhancement**: Improve visual impact

#### Color Grading
1. **Brand Color Accuracy**: Match exact brand colors
2. **Skin Tone Correction**: Natural flesh tones in lifestyle shots
3. **Product Color Fidelity**: Accurate material representation
4. **Background Neutrality**: Clean, non-distracting backgrounds
5. **Consistency**: Matching look across all images

### Retouching Standards

#### Product Cleanup
- **Dust and Scratches**: Remove manufacturing imperfections
- **Reflections**: Control unwanted reflections and glare
- **Background**: Perfect seamless backgrounds
- **Shadows**: Natural, consistent shadow placement

#### Enhancement Guidelines
- **Sharpening**: Appropriate for intended use (web vs. print)
- **Noise Reduction**: Clean images without over-processing
- **Color Enhancement**: Subtle improvements, maintain realism
- **Composition**: Minor perspective corrections if needed

### File Preparation

#### Web Optimization
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI for web use
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for fast loading
- **Format**: JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Color Space**: sRGB for web compatibility

#### Print Preparation
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Color Space**: Adobe RGB or CMYK for print
- **File Format**: TIFF or high-quality JPEG
- **Bleed**: 0.125" bleed for printed materials

#### Social Media Formats
- **Instagram Square**: 1080x1080 pixels
- **Instagram Story**: 1080x1920 pixels
- **Facebook Cover**: 1200x630 pixels
- **Twitter Header**: 1500x500 pixels
- **LinkedIn**: 1200x627 pixels

## Campaign-Specific Photography

### Pre-Launch Photography

#### Teaser Images
- **Silhouettes**: Product shape without revealing details
- **Close-up Details**: Intriguing features without context
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Development and testing process
- **Comparison Setup**: Problem vs. solution staging

#### Email Marketing Images
- **Header Graphics**: 600px wide, brand-consistent
- **Product Highlights**: Square format for mobile
- **Call-to-Action**: Visual elements supporting CTAs
- **Social Proof**: Customer photos and testimonials

### Live Campaign Photography

#### Campaign Page Images
- **Hero Image**: Primary campaign visual
- **Feature Gallery**: 8-12 detailed product shots
- **Lifestyle Gallery**: Real-world usage scenarios
- **Technical Specs**: Dimensional and specification images

#### Social Media Content
- **Daily Posts**: Variety of angles and contexts
- **Story Content**: Behind-the-scenes, user-generated
- **Video Thumbnails**: Compelling stills from video content
- **User-Generated**: Customer photos and unboxings

### Advertising Photography

#### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- **Square Format**: 1080x1080 for feed posts
- **Vertical Format**: 1080x1350 for mobile optimization
- **Carousel Images**: Consistent style across multiple images
- **Video Thumbnails**: Engaging first frame for video ads

#### Google Ads
- **Product Images**: Clean, white background preferred
- **Lifestyle Context**: For display network ads
- **Comparison Shots**: Before/after or vs. competitor
- **Multiple Angles**: Various perspectives for shopping ads

- [ ] **Style**: Matches established brand aesthetic
- [ ] **Colors**: Accurate brand color reproduction
- [ ] **Mood**: Appropriate for brand personality
- [ ] **Quality**: Professional standard throughout

### Campaign Effectiveness
- [ ] **Clarity**: Product features clearly visible
- [ ] **Appeal**: Visually compelling and engaging

- [ ] **Context**: Appropriate for target audience
- [ ] **Versatility**: Works across multiple platforms

## Budget Planning

### Photography Investment Levels

#### Basic Package ($2,000-5,000)
- **Scope**: 20-30 images, basic lighting setup
- **Equipment**: Entry-level DSLR, basic lighting
- **Retouching**: Basic color correction and cleanup
- **Deliverables**: Web-optimized files

#### Professional Package ($5,000-15,000)
- **Scope**: 50-75 images, multiple lighting setups
- **Equipment**: Professional cameras, studio lighting
- **Retouching**: Advanced retouching and color grading
- **Deliverables**: Web and print files, multiple formats

#### Premium Package ($15,000-30,000)
- **Scope**: 100+ images, lifestyle and studio shots
- **Equipment**: High-end cameras, professional studio
- **Retouching**: Extensive retouching and creative enhancement
- **Deliverables**: Complete asset library, all formats

### Cost Optimization Strategies

#### DIY Approach
- **Equipment Rental**: Rent professional gear for shoot days
- **Online Tutorials**: Learn basic photography and lighting
- **Software**: Use free or low-cost editing software
- **Templates**: Follow established shot lists and setups

#### Hybrid Approach
- **Professional Hero Shots**: Hire pro for key images
- **DIY Lifestyle**: Shoot casual usage photos yourself
- **Batch Processing**: Efficient workflow for multiple images
- **Template Reuse**: Consistent setup for efficiency
