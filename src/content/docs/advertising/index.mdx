# Advertising & Paid Media

The advertising section provides strategic frameworks and creative assets for paid media campaigns across multiple platforms. These resources help you build effective advertising funnels that drive qualified traffic and conversions.

## Advertising Strategy Framework

### Campaign Objectives
**Awareness Stage**
- Brand introduction and education
- Problem/solution awareness
- Audience expansion and reach

**Consideration Stage**
- Product demonstration and benefits
- Social proof and testimonials
- Comparison and differentiation

**Conversion Stage**
- Direct response and sales
- Urgency and scarcity messaging
- Retargeting and remarketing

### Platform-Specific Strategies

#### Facebook & Instagram Advertising
- **Audience Targeting**: Interest-based, lookalike, and custom audiences
- **Creative Formats**: Video ads, carousel ads, collection ads, story ads
- **Campaign Types**: Traffic, conversions, lead generation, brand awareness
- **Optimization**: Pixel implementation, conversion tracking, A/B testing

#### Google Ads
- **Search Campaigns**: Keyword targeting, ad extensions, landing page optimization
- **Display Network**: Visual ads, audience targeting, remarketing lists
- **YouTube Advertising**: Video ads, TrueView campaigns, bumper ads
- **Shopping Campaigns**: Product listings, merchant center optimization

#### Other Platforms
- **LinkedIn**: B2B targeting, professional audience engagement
- **Twitter**: Real-time engagement, trending topics, promoted tweets
- **TikTok**: Short-form video content, younger demographics
- **Pinterest**: Visual discovery, lifestyle targeting
