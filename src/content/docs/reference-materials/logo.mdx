## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Do's
✅ **Maintain Clear Space**
- Keep minimum clear space around logo
- Use 1x logo height as clear space measurement

✅ **Use Approved Colors**
- Primary Blue: #0066CC
- Secondary Orange: #FF6B35
- Black: #000000
- White: #FFFFFF

✅ **Maintain Proportions**
- Never stretch or distort the logo
- Always scale proportionally

✅ **Use High-Quality Files**
- Vector formats (SVG) for scalability
- High-resolution PNG for raster needs

### Don'ts
❌ **Never Distort**
- Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- Don't change proportions

❌ **Never Change Colors**
- Don't use unauthorized colors
- Don't apply gradients or effects

❌ **Never Crowd the Logo**
- Don't place text or elements too close
- Respect minimum clear space

❌ **Never Use Low-Quality Files**
- Don't use pixelated or blurry versions
- Don't use unauthorized recreations
