## Checklist Templates by Industry

### Technology & Hardware
- Focus on technical validation and manufacturing
- Emphasize early adopter engagement
- Include regulatory compliance checks
- Plan for international shipping complexity

### Software & Digital Products
- Prioritize beta testing and user feedback
- Leverage developer and tech communities
- Focus on Product Hunt and tech media
- Plan for immediate digital delivery

### Creative & Media Projects
- Build on existing fan base and community
- Emphasize storytelling and emotional connection
- Leverage creator networks and cross-promotion
- Plan for physical and digital reward fulfillment

### Fashion & Lifestyle
- Focus on visual content and influencer partnerships
- Emphasize social media and visual platforms
- Include sizing and fit considerations
- Plan for seasonal timing and trends

### Games & Entertainment
- Engage gaming communities and forums
- Leverage playtesting and community feedback
- Focus on gameplay videos and demonstrations
- Plan for manufacturing and distribution logistics

### Food & Beverage
- Include regulatory and safety compliance
- Focus on taste testing and sampling
- Emphasize local and regional marketing
- Plan for perishability and shipping constraints
