### Stage 1: Early Adopters (Days 1-7)

#### Target Metrics
```markdown

## FUNDING GOALS
- **Target Funding**: 30-40% of total goal
- **Target Backers**: 25-35% of total backers
- **Average Pledge**: $[X] (typically higher than later stages)
- **Daily Growth Rate**: 3-5% of total goal per day

## TRAFFIC GOALS
- **Daily Visitors**: [X] minimum
- **Conversion Rate**: 5-8% (higher due to early adopter enthusiasm)

  - Direct: 40-50%
  - Social Media: 25-35%

## ENGAGEMENT GOALS
- **Email Open Rate**: 35-45%
- **Email Click Rate**: 8-12%
```
