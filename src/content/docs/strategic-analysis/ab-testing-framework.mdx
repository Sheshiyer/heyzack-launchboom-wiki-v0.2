# A/B Testing Framework

A comprehensive framework for testing and optimizing every aspect of your crowdfunding campaign to maximize conversions and ROI.

## A/B Testing Fundamentals

### Test Planning Process
1. **Hypothesis Formation**
2. **Test Design**
3. **Implementation**
4. **Data Collection**
5. **Analysis & Decision**
6. **Implementation**

### Statistical Requirements
- **Minimum Sample Size**: 1,000 visitors per variant
- **Test Duration**: Minimum 7 days
- **Confidence Level**: 95%
- **Statistical Power**: 80%
- **Minimum Detectable Effect**: 10%

## Campaign Page Testing

### Hero Section Tests

#### Test 1: Headline Variations
- **Hypothesis**: Benefit-focused headlines convert better than feature-focused
- **Variant A**: "Revolutionary Smart Home System"
- **Variant B**: "Save 30% on Energy Bills with AI"
- **Metric**: Click-through rate to video
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Sample Size**: 2,000 visitors

#### Test 2: Call-to-Action Button
- **Hypothesis**: Urgency-based CTAs increase conversions
- **Variant A**: "Back This Project"
- **Variant B**: "Join 1,000+ Early Backers"
- **Metric**: Conversion to backer
- **Duration**: 14 days
- **Sample Size**: 5,000 visitors

### Video Testing

#### Test 3: Video Thumbnail
- **Hypothesis**: Product-focused thumbnails get more plays
- **Variant A**: Founder speaking
- **Variant B**: Product in action
- **Metric**: Video play rate
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Sample Size**: 3,000 visitors

#### Test 4: Video Length
- **Hypothesis**: Shorter videos have higher completion rates
- **Variant A**: 3-minute video
- **Variant B**: 90-second video
- **Metric**: Video completion rate
- **Duration**: 10 days
- **Sample Size**: 2,500 visitors

### Pricing Tests

#### Test 5: Early Bird Pricing
- **Hypothesis**: Higher discount increases early conversions
- **Variant A**: 20% early bird discount
- **Variant B**: 35% early bird discount
- **Metric**: Early bird conversion rate
- **Duration**: First 48 hours
- **Sample Size**: 1,500 visitors

#### Test 6: Pricing Display
- **Hypothesis**: Showing savings increases perceived value
- **Variant A**: "$299 (Early Bird)"
- **Variant B**: "$299 (Save $150)"
- **Metric**: Pledge conversion rate
- **Duration**: 7 days
- **Sample Size**: 4,000 visitors

## Best Practices

### Test Design
- Test one element at a time
- Ensure sufficient sample size
- Run tests for full business cycles
- Account for external factors
- Document everything

### Statistical Rigor
- Use proper significance testing
- Avoid peeking at results early
- Consider practical significance
- Account for multiple testing
- Validate with follow-up tests

### Implementation
- Act on significant results
- Share learnings across team
- Build testing culture
- Continuously iterate
- Measure long-term impact
