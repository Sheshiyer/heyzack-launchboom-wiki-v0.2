# Porter's Five Forces Analysis

A comprehensive competitive analysis using Porter's Five Forces framework to assess market dynamics and competitive positioning for your crowdfunding campaign.

## Framework Overview

Porter's Five Forces helps analyze the competitive environment and identify strategic opportunities and threats in your market.

### The Five Forces
1. **Threat of New Entrants**
2. **Bargaining Power of Suppliers**
3. **Bargaining Power of Buyers**
4. **Threat of Substitute Products**
5. **Competitive Rivalry**
