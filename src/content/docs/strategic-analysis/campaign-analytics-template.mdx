## Key Metrics Definitions

### Conversion Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who become backers
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Total marketing spend ÷ number of backers
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue ÷ advertising spend
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average total value per backer

### Engagement Metrics
- **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of single-page sessions
- **Time on Page**: Average time spent on campaign page
- **Pages per Session**: Average pages viewed per visit
- **Social Shares**: Number of times content is shared
