## Risk Management

### Potential Risks & Mitigation

#### Low Initial Momentum
- **Risk**: Slow start in first 48 hours
- **Mitigation**: Larger email list, stronger launch day push
- **Contingency**: Emergency PR outreach, influencer activation

#### High Customer Acquisition Costs
- **Risk**: CAC exceeds budget
- **Mitigation**: Focus on organic channels, optimize ad targeting
- **Contingency**: Reduce ad spend, increase organic efforts

#### Competitive Response
- **Risk**: Competitor launches similar product
- **Mitigation**: Strong differentiation, first-mover advantage
- **Contingency**: Adjust positioning, emphasize unique benefits

#### Technical Issues
- **Risk**: Campaign page or payment issues
- **Mitigation**: Thorough testing, backup systems
- **Contingency**: Quick response team, alternative solutions
